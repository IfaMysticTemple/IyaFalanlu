About Iya Falanlu

Iya Falanlu is an Iyanifa and High Priestess of Orunmila, and an Oshun Priestess. She is also a master loctician, a mother, and a grandmother. Her path is devotion to alignment with Ori, beauty in discipline, and compassion in service. Her teachings emphasize contemplative practice, gentle ritual, and the cultivation of inner stillness.

Meditative Teachings & Practices

  • Aligning with Ori — Centering prayer and head alignment.
  • Breath of Stillness — Fourfold breath for calm and clarity.
  • Eshu Meditation — Opening the crossroads with respect and humility.
  • Daily Calm Invocation — A brief morning and evening recitation.
  • Irosun Ofun Iré Reflection — Writing practice to anchor blessings.

Suggested Practice Rhythm

Morning: Breath of Stillness (5–10 min) • Aligning with Ori (3–5 min)
Midday: Short gratitude pause (1–2 min)
Evening: Eshu Meditation or Daily Calm Invocation (5–10 min)
